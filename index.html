İlke Çuğu

I am a Software Engineer at Microsoft working on distributed databases. Prior to joining Microsoft, I was a Research Scientist in the Explainable Machine Learning Group at the University of Tübingen under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Zeynep Akata. Before that, I was a Software Engineer at Siemens, working in the Smart Infrastructure Department. I received my BSc (2016) and MSc (2018) degrees from Middle East Technical University in Computer Engineering, advised by Prof. Dr. Murat Manguoğlu and Asst. Prof. Dr. Emre Akbaş.
